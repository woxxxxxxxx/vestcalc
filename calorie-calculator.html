Frequently Asked Questions

Is Calorie Calculator free to use?
Yes, completely free. Calorie Calculator requires no account, no subscription, and no payment. Use it as often as you like to track your health metrics.
How accurate is Calorie Calculator?
Calorie Calculator uses standard medical and scientific formulas widely used by healthcare professionals. Results are general estimates; individual results can vary based on factors not captured by the formula.
Should I use Calorie Calculator results to make medical decisions?
Calorie Calculator provides useful general health metrics, but results should not replace professional medical advice. Consult your doctor or healthcare provider for personalized guidance.
Can I use Calorie Calculator for multiple people?
Yes. Calorie Calculator works for any individual; simply enter the relevant measurements for each person. No data is saved between uses, so each calculation is independent.
Is my health data stored when I use Calorie Calculator?
No. All calculations in Calorie Calculator happen locally in your browser. No personal health information you enter is sent to or stored on any server.
