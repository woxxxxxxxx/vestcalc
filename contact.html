Frequently Asked Questions

Are all calculators really free?
Yes, every calculator on VestCalc is completely free with no sign-up required and no hidden costs.
Do you store my calculation data?
No. All calculations happen in your browser using JavaScript. Your inputs are never sent to our servers.
How accurate are the results?
Our calculators use standard financial formulas and are designed to be accurate. However, results are estimates and should not replace advice from a qualified financial professional.
Can I suggest a new calculator?
Absolutely! Use the contact form above and select "Feature Request." We read every suggestion.
How quickly do you respond?
We aim to respond to all messages within 2 business days.
