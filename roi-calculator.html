Frequently Asked Questions

Is ROI Calculator free to use?
Yes, completely free. ROI Calculator requires no account and has no usage limits. Model as many investment scenarios as you need at no cost.
How accurate are results from ROI Calculator?
ROI Calculator uses verified financial formulas. All results are projections based on the assumptions you enter and should complement, not replace, professional financial advice.
Can I model different return rates in ROI Calculator?
Yes. ROI Calculator lets you adjust expected annual return rates, contribution amounts, and time horizons so you can compare outcomes under different market scenarios.
Does ROI Calculator factor in taxes on investment gains?
ROI Calculator focuses on pre-tax growth calculations. For tax-adjusted projections, consider entering after-tax return estimates or consult a tax professional for your specific situation.
Is my data safe when I use ROI Calculator?
Yes. All calculations in ROI Calculator run locally in your browser. No data you enter is ever transmitted to or stored on any server.
