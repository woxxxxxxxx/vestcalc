Frequently Asked Questions

How do weighted grades work?
Each assignment category (homework, tests, final) has a percentage weight. Your score in each category is multiplied by its weight and summed. Example: 90% on homework (worth 30%) + 80% on exams (worth 70%) = 0.9×30 + 0.8×70 = 83% overall.
What if my weights don't add up to 100%?
This calculator normalizes whatever weights you enter, so partial completion works fine. If you only know some of your grades, enter only those and the result reflects your current standing in graded work so far.
How do I convert percentage to letter grade?
Common scale: A+ ≥97%, A ≥93%, A- ≥90%, B+ ≥87%, B ≥83%, B- ≥80%, C+ ≥77%, C ≥73%, C- ≥70%, D ≥60%, F below 60%. Your school may use a different scale — check your syllabus.
Can I calculate what I need on my final exam?
Yes — use our Final Grade Calculator which is specifically designed to tell you the minimum score you need on your remaining exam to achieve your target grade.
